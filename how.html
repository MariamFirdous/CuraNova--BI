How it works

How CuraNova reads a report

Step-by-step flow
Read

It finds the clinical terms

Test names, abbreviations, and reference ranges are picked out from the surrounding text.

Translate

It swaps jargon for plain language

Each term is matched to a short, everyday explanation of what it is and why it's measured.

Flag

It flags what's outside the normal range

Values marked high, low, or abnormal are called out clearly, without causing alarm.

CuraNova — built to make reports readable, not to make decisions for you.
